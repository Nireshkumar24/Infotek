Company's history, mission, and values.

A Journey of Innovation Since 2018:

Infotek, a prominent player in the healthcare industry, had its humble beginnings in Vellore in August 2018. Founded and led by Gokul Nagalingam, the CEO, the company embarked on its journey with a small yet dedicated team of just 5 members.

Reforming Revenue Cycle Management:

Infotek has scaled new heights, boasting a robust workforce of over 200 professionals. The company's journey began with a strategic focus on the US market, where it has built a reputation as a trusted partner for Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) services, providing exceptional support to a growing number of clients. The evolution of Infotek reflects not only its commitment to excellence but also its unwavering dedication to transforming the healthcare landscape.

Our Mission:

To empower healthcare providers to achieve financial excellence by delivering world-class Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) solutions tailored to the unique needs of the U.S. healthcare market. We are committed to optimizing revenue streams, reducing operational burdens, and enhancing patient care through innovation and exceptional service.

Our Vision:

To be the trusted partner of choice for healthcare providers, serving the U.S. market, and setting the gold standard for Revenue Cycle Management. We aspire to drive positive change in the healthcare industry by seamlessly integrating cutting-edge technology, industry expertise, and personalized service to unlock the full financial potential of our clients.

Profiles of key team members and their expertise.

About the CEO: Gokul Nagalingam

Gokul Nagalingam is the visionary Founder and CEO of Infotek Healthcare Solutions. Holding a degree in Nursing, he is a certified Coding Specialist affiliated with AAPC, recognized by the U.S. Government in 2013. Gokul's fervor for cutting-edge medical technologies has driven him to continuously refine his expertise in medical coding techniques, amassing over a decade of hands-on experience in the field.

As a dedicated member of prestigious healthcare associations, including the American Health Information Management Association (AHIMA) and the American Academy of Professional Coders (AAPC), Gokul is well-versed in various medical disciplines. His proficiency extends across Medical Coding, Billing, Auditing, and AAPC practices.

Gokul's commitment to client satisfaction and exceptional client management skills have allowed him to establish a global presence, with a clientele spanning the United States, Canada, and beyond. With a profusion of 15 years of experience, his comprehensive knowledge extends to various healthcare domains, including HCC, ED, E&M, IP DRG, Home Health Care, Ambulatory Coding, IVR, and Denial Coding.

Under his expert leadership, Infotek Healthcare is poised to revolutionize Revenue Cycle Management, ensuring clients' financial prosperity and superior healthcare services.
